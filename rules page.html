Rules for advertising

Last updated: May 11, 2025

General guidelines for acceptable content

The website you are advertising CANNOT UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES contain:

  1. Piracy and unauthorized Content

    2. Content related to pirated software, cracks, emulators, or unauthorized usage of copyrighted material.

  2. Adult content

    3. Including pornography, explicit sexual material, or any direct or indirect links to adult sites.

  3. Illegal content

    4. Content promoting or selling hazardous substances, illegal drugs, counterfeit products, weapons, or any products/services that violate intellectual property rights, or break any laws.

  4. Violence, gore, and harmful content

    5. Content that depicts, promotes, or glorifies violence, death, gore, terrorism, or criminal activity.

  5. Hate speech and discrimination

    6. Content that promotes or endorses racism, sexism, religious hate, gender discrimination, or other forms of hate speech.

  6. Profanity and offensive language

    7. Content containing explicit language, slurs, or discriminatory/derogatory remarks against any individual or group.

  7. Spammy content and non-comprehensible submissions

    8. Submissions containing spam, gibberish (e.g., "nkejrbvkdn"), or content that doesn’t make sense will be rejected immediately.

  8. Misleading claims and "Too good to be true" offers

    9. Content that includes false, unverifiable claims such as "earn a million dollars in three days with little effort," or other deceptive advertising tactics.

  9. Creatives containing misleading information

    10. Ads with phrases like “your software is outdated,” “your device is infected,” or any other form of misleading, alarmist, or deceptive claims will be rejected.

  10. Malware, phishing, and harmful software

    11. Ads that contain malware, phishing links, or any downloadable content that could damage or compromise a user's system.

  11. Privacy and data protection violations

    12. Ads must comply with applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) and must not collect personal data without user consent.

  12. Open content areas with inadequate moderation

    13. Ads leading to forums, chat rooms, or user-generated content areas that lack sufficient moderation to ensure compliance with these rules.

I reserve the right to update these guidelines as well as terminating any ad campaigns which does not comply with these rules (even after approval and on-going campaigns), with NO refunds and NO compensation for lost revenue.

I use a 5-strike rule on ad buying requests (meaning if you break the rules 5 times, you WILL BE permabanned and will not be able to buy ad spaces anymore on my site). You WILL BE placed on a banned list.

Please obey the rules, so we all can have a pleasant time on the blog :)
